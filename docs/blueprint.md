# **App Name**: Nexus CRM

## Core Features:

- Live Pulse Dashboard: Visualizing real-time conversion rates, revenue trends, and activity metrics with beautiful charts.
- Firestore Contact Engine: Enterprise-grade lead and customer management with optimized Firestore document schemas and real-time state synchronization.
- Pipeline Flowboard: Drag-and-drop Kanban board for managing the sales funnel from discovery to closing, providing clear visual tracking of deal values.
- Smart Document Vault: Secure storage and management of contracts, quotations, and agreements using Firebase Storage integration.
- Task & Follow-up Command: Calendar-integrated task management that logs every interaction and triggers reminders for meetings.
- Activity Timeline: A comprehensive history of all call records, emails, and notes for every relationship.
- Next-Best-Action tool: An AI tool that reasons through interaction history and pipeline status to generate strategic follow-up suggestions.

## Style Guidelines:

- The scheme follows a professional Dark mode for reduced eye strain during deep work. Primary color: Sophisticated Deep Indigo (#4D79F2). Background: Desaturated Dark Slate (#16181C). Accent: Cyan-Blue (#5ECDF9) to highlight critical success paths.
- Font pairing of 'Space Grotesk' for modern, tech-forward headlines and 'Inter' for highly readable data-rich tables and body text.
- Thinline SVG icons that are precise and minimalist, avoiding visual clutter in complex data views.
- Standard SaaS sidebar navigation with a high-density 'Control Center' layout for maximum data visibility.
- Fluid, micro-interactions for Kanban movements and hover-state escalations to make the interface feel tactile and responsive.
